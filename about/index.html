Our Story

Built for brands that
mean business

Ninearc Agency was founded with a single belief: that world-class marketing shouldn't be reserved for billion-dollar companies. Every business deserves a brand and marketing strategy that commands attention.

We started in Canada and have grown into a full-service integrated agency handling everything from social media and brand identity to experiential marketing and web development. Our clients trust us because we treat their business like our own.

The name "Ninearc" reflects our philosophy — multiple disciplines, one arc of growth. Everything we do is connected, intentional, and built to compound over time.
